Raspberry Lemon Waffles

Sweet, tart, tasty! These waffles are sure to wake you up with how easy and delicious they are.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 lemons zested
  • 1/2 cup raspberry jam

Steps

  1. Preheat the waffle iron on medium.
  2. Sift together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
  3. In a seperate bowl whisk together milk, egg yolks, vanilla, and lemon zest.
  4. Pour liquid ingredients into the dry ones andd mix gently until partly combined.
  5. Add melted butter and continue mixing until fully combined.
  6. Beat egg whites in a seperate bowl until stiff before foolding them into the batter gently.
  7. Pour batter into waffle iron and cook until golden brown.
  8. Top with raspberry jam to taste.
  9. Enjoy!
